Caringbytes

Caringbytes is the Caringbah High School Coding club, run in TF.15 on Mondays at lunch. Come and hang out with other coders who are just as obsessed interested in coding as you are.

- - -

It [Caringbytes] is an excellent opportunity for those who share a passion for coding to get together and discuss personal projects, work on a current club project or even learn to code from scratch! (no pun intended)
