About Us

Caringbytes was started in 2017 by Gabriel Toh because he thought Caringbah High School should have a coding club. It has been supervised by many teachers over time, in many different rooms, but currently it's held in TF.15 on Monday Lunch times, supervised by Mr Yeung.

Leaders and Skill Levels

Caringbytes wouldn't be running without the help of its leaders however, who currently are Oliver Lai, Hamish Magill, and Ethan Phillips.

In Caringbytes, you can code whatever you want. We welcome people of all skill levels, from Scratch , to assembly code .

I don't think anyone at Caringbytes actually knows assembly code, but if you do, then great!
