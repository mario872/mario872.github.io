Our Community

Welcoming Community

Our community at Caringbytes is very welcoming. We want anyone who's interested in coding to come to Caringbytes and enjoy coding with other like-minded souls.

If you need help, just ask - all of the leaders would be very happy to help you.

Unstructured Chill Coding Time

Caringbytes doesn't have a set structure, we just meet in one place, then sit down, eat, and code for 40 minutes before going to our next class.

It's so chill you wouldn't even know it was a club.
